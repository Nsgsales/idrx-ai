Our Mission

Built for the Providers
Who Deserve Better.

Healthcare reimbursement has become increasingly complex. We're here to change that.

Healthcare reimbursement has become increasingly complex.

Providers deliver essential care every day—yet navigating the payment side of healthcare has become a full-time job in itself. Payers have entire teams dedicated to minimizing reimbursement. Providers often don't have the same resources to fight back.

The No Surprises Act and the Independent Dispute Resolution process changed that. For the first time, providers have a structured federal mechanism to challenge underpayments. But the process is still complex, time-consuming, and documentation-intensive.

That's why we built idrx.ai. We believe every provider—from a solo emergency physician to a large health system—deserves technology that helps them navigate IDR efficiently, accurately, and confidently.

Work With Us

Our mission is simple.

  • Reduce administrative burden through intelligent automation
  • Level the playing field between providers and payers
